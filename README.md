## Overview

Liquid Rhythm is designed for building, adjusting, and exporting drum and rhythm ideas in a workflow that works both as a standalone creative tool and as part of a plugin-based production setup. The emphasis is on quick pattern generation, adaptable sequencing, and tactile control, making it easier to move from an empty session to a practical groove without a complicated setup.

It works well for producers inside a DAW and for users who prefer a separate environment for sketching rhythm ideas before sending them out as MIDI. With modular sequencing, real-time modulation, and AI-assisted rhythm generation, the app offers several approaches to constructing parts that can then be edited, reshaped, and folded into a wider production process.

## How to Use It

A straightforward workflow is:

1. Start a new pattern in standalone mode or inside your DAW.
2. Choose a base rhythm and let the generator propose variations.
3. Adjust the polymetric grid to change how layers interact.
4. Use velocity morphing and real-time modulation to shape movement.
5. Add ghost note detail where needed.
6. Export the result as MIDI or keep refining inside the plugin session.

For DAW-centered work, insert the plugin on a track, trigger or draw patterns, and then route the generated MIDI to your drum instrument or sampler. For standalone sessions, sketch ideas first and bring them into your production environment afterward.
